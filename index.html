About Us

Medihelp Hospitals stands as a prominent healthcare provider in Sri Lanka, recognized for its excellence. With a history spanning over four decades, we have significantly expanded our network of medical facilities. Our services now encompass inpatient and outpatient care, laboratories and sample collection centers, pharmacies, and medical imaging units. Medihelp is committed to a patient-centric approach, emphasizing affordability, accessibility, and the provision of comprehensive facilities coupled with top-tier medical expertise. Cardiology

Medihelp Hospital offers comprehensive cardiology treatments, including diagnostics, intervention, and rehabilitation, providing patients with personalized care for heart-related conditions.

The hospital’s cardiology department is equipped with advanced technology and skilled professionals, ensuring effective management of cardiovascular diseases.

Neurology

Medihelp Hospital offers specialized neurology treatments with advanced diagnostic tools and expert neurologists, ensuring high-quality care for neurological disorders.

At Medihelp Hospital, patients receive comprehensive neurology care. The hospital’s skilled neurologists utilize state-of-the-art technology to diagnose and treat a wide range of neurological conditions effectively and compassionately.

Hepatology

Medihelp Hospital offers a range of advanced hepatology treatments, including liver disease management, transplantation, and specialized care for conditions like hepatitis and cirrhosis, ensuring comprehensive liver health care.

With experienced hepatologists and state-of-the-art facilities, Medihelp Hospital provides personalized treatment plans, focusing on patient-centered care and innovative therapies for liver diseases, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life.

Pediatrics

MediHelp Hospital offers a wide range of pediatric treatments, ensuring that children receive the specialized care they need for various health conditions, from routine check-ups to complex illnesses.

With a team of experienced pediatricians, MediHelp Hospital is dedicated to providing comprehensive care tailored to the unique needs of each child, focusing on their growth, development, and overall well-being.

Eye Care

MediHelp Hospital offers specialized eye care treatments, including cataract surgery and glaucoma management, ensuring patients receive comprehensive care tailored to their unique vision needs.

With state-of-the-art technology and experienced ophthalmologists, MediHelp Hospital provides top-quality eye care services, from routine check-ups to advanced surgical procedures, prioritizing patient safety and comfort.

Frequently Asked Questions

Guiding you on your journey to a heathier you with the right experts

What services does MediHelp offer?

MediHelp provides a wide range of medical services, including consultations, diagnostics, surgeries, and specialized treatments in various fields such as cardiology, dermatology, and eye care.

What are the visiting hours for patients?

Visiting hours may vary depending on the unit and specific patient needs. Please check our website or call the hospital for the most up-to-date visiting hours.

Is there a pharmacy on-site?

Yes, MediHelp has an on-site pharmacy that provides prescription medications and over-the-counter products for your convenience.

How can I provide feedback about my experience?

We welcome your feedback! You can submit your comments through our website's contact form or speak to our patient services representative during your visit.

Are there any dietary options available for inpatients?

Yes, MediHelp offers a variety of dietary options to cater to different needs and preferences. Patients can discuss their dietary requirements with our nutritionists during their stay.

What insurance plans are accepted?

MediHelp accepts various insurance plans. Please check our website or contact our billing department for a list of accepted insurance providers and coverage details.
